Lonwabo Guard and Security Services is a well established and reputable security company. Our intention is to establish our company as the leading guard and Security Company in South Africa. We aim to be the recognized leader in providing quality accredited security services to satisfy individual client needs. Built on the premise that security and safety in our country is in a downward spiral, Lonwabo Guard and Security Services strives to be a major contributing factor in correcting this trend. Lonwabo Guard and Security Services was started by Butholezwe Mhlophe whose intention was to establish a security company that would offer a unique and tailored service to its clientele. Lonwabo Guard and Security Services’ integrated security model incorporates the manpower, technology and superior management strategy necessary to achieve this tailored security services for its clientele. Our staff is highly trained and skilled in ensuring the protection of our clients, their families and their assets.

We know that our staff represent our company on a daily basis and our code of ethic, company policies and procedures will ensure that they are presentable, efcient and at all times ready to serve our clients. Within legal requirements, our employees will go to all lengths to protect and serve our clients and their communities. We are committed to exceeding the expectations of our clients by consistently providing an exceptional level of customer service. We strive to maintain consistency in the delivery of our services, to continually meet the specic needs of each of our customers, and to foster company values within each of our guards.
